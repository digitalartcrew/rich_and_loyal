The Label

More Than
A Label.

Rich & Loyal is an independent hip-hop label founded in the Bronx, New York. Built on the principle that authentic music speaks for itself, the label is home to artists who carry the Bronx legacy with pride and push the culture forward.

From street anthems to introspective storytelling, the Rich & Loyal family represents every side of the Bronx experience — the grind, the glory, the loyalty that holds it all together.

Artist Spotlight

Rich Mo.

Rich Mo is the CEO and founder of Rich & Loyal, and one of the Bronx's most authentic voices in hip-hop. Blending street narratives with sharp lyricism, he built an imprint that reflects both the raw reality and the ambition of New York City.

With releases spanning from A Bronx Tale Circa 2011 to the Money Talk EP, Rich Mo has proven that loyalty to the craft and the community pays dividends. No features needed — the bars do all the talking.
